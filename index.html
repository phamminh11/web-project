About us

We are FTC Team 24751 - GreenAms Robotics Team, a non-profit FIRST Tech Challenge team of high-school students from Ha Noi Amsterdam High school for the Gifted

GreenAms Robotics Team seeks to INNOVATE each year by approaching challenges in creative ways, ITERATE by consistently working to improve designs and strategies, and to ILLUSTRATE by having measurable impact on our members as they are inspired by science and technology

Our Mission

The mission of FIRST is to inspire young peope to be STEM leaders and innovators, by engaging them in exciting mentor-based programs that build science, engineering, and technology skills, that inspire innovation and foster well-rounded life skills including self-confidience, communication, and leadership

FIRST

The FIRST Tech Challenge (FTC) engages students in exciting mentor-based programs that build STEM skills, inspire innovation, and foster self-confidence and leadership.

STEM

FIRST and teams like FTC2471, GreenAms Robotics team, exist to inspire students to pursue college majors and careers in STEM: science, technology, engineering and math.
